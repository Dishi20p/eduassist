Able Assist plays its role by collecting and disseminating data on education-related resources for differently-abled children. Consolidating this information on a singular platform to make it easier for parents, caregivers, and educators to access the resources and support they need. By providing information on both rights and resources, Able Assist contributes to raising awareness about the available opportunities and assistance. This not only helps individuals directly involved with differently-abled children but also contributes to creating a more informed and supportive society.

Parents, educators, and caregivers can find practical tools, inclusive teaching methods, and information on adaptive technologies to support students throughout their academic journey.

The content spans various subjects and addresses concerns related to creating inclusive learning environments.
